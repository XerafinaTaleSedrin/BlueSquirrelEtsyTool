# 5-Color Golos Shrines (60 Cards)
**Format**: Historic / Timeless
**Archetype**: 5-Color Enchantment Combo with Protection Shell
**Win Condition**: Exponential shrine triggers — drain, damage, tokens, +1/+1 counters — protected by hexproof + shroud so opponents can't interact

---

## The Game Plan

```
TURNS 1-3: Ramp with basics + card draw engine
  Sythis (GW) ──→ every enchantment you cast draws a card + gains 1 life
  Path to the World Tree (1G) ──→ enchantment, finds any land (Sythis draws off this!)
  Cultivate (2G) ──→ basic to field + basic to hand (plenty of basics to find)
  Sterling Grove (GW) ──→ all enchantments gain shroud (drops early, protects everything)

TURN 4-5: Golos + Protection
  Golos (5 colorless) ──→ ETB fetches The World Tree
  Shalai (3W) ──→ you + all your creatures gain hexproof
  Now at 6+ lands, World Tree taps for ANY color

TURN 5-6: Sanctum of All online
  Sanctum of All (WUBRG) ──→ free shrine every upkeep (protected by Sterling Grove shroud)
  Opponents can't target your shrines OR your creatures

TURN 6+: Untouchable shrine avalanche
  Life's Origin ──→ exponential shrine tokens
  Shalai ──→ hexproof on all creatures (including shrine creature tokens)
  Sterling Grove ──→ shroud on all enchantments (shrines + Path + Sterling itself)
  Sigarda ──→ flying hexproof + can't be forced to sacrifice
  Sphere of Safety ──→ creatures can't attack you (cost X per attacker, X = enchantments)
  Sythis ──→ every shrine cast draws a card + gains life (fuels the engine)
  NOTHING can be targeted. Only mass exile (Farewell) beats this board.
```

---

## The Protection Shell

This is what makes the deck Diamond-viable. Three layers of protection:

| Layer | Card | Protects | From |
|-------|------|----------|------|
| 1 | Sterling Grove (GW) | All enchantments (= all shrines) | Targeted removal, bounce, exile |
| 2 | Shalai, Voice of Plenty (3W) | You + all creatures | Targeted anything — burn, removal, hand disruption |
| 3 | Sigarda, Host of Herons (2GWW) | You | Forced sacrifice effects (Edict, Liliana, etc.) |

**With all three out**: Shrines have shroud (can't be targeted). Creatures have hexproof (can't be targeted). You can't be forced to sacrifice. The ONLY answers are untargeted board wipes (Wrath of God, Farewell) — and Go-Shintai of Life's Origin recurs shrines from graveyard after wipes.

## Why Basics + World Tree

Triomes enter tapped and slow you down. This build instead:
- **17 basics** = Cultivate always finds 2 targets, Fabled Passage always has options
- **4x World Tree** = once you hit 6 lands (turn 4-5 with ramp), ALL lands tap for any color
- **Golos fetches World Tree** = guaranteed color fixing by turn 5
- Faster early game: basics enter untapped, you curve Path → Cultivate → Golos without a single tapped land
- Consistent mulligans: any hand with Forest + ramp spell is keepable

## How It Plays

### Opening Hand Priority
**Keep**: 2-3 lands (must include Forest or Fabled Passage) + ramp (Cultivate or Path)
**Great keeps**: Forest + Path + Cultivate + any shrine = you're on track for turn 5 Golos
**Mulligan**: No green source, no ramp, 5+ lands, or protection but no shrines

### Turns 1-3: Ramp + Early Protection
- **Turn 2**: Path to the World Tree (enchantment, ramps) or Sterling Grove (protects everything after). Cheap shrine if you have the color (Boundless Vigor off Forest, Stone Fangs off Swamp)
- **Turn 3**: Cultivate. Always Cultivate if you can — getting to 5 lands for Golos is priority #1
- Sterling Grove on 2 means every shrine you play from now on has shroud immediately

### Turns 4-5: Golos + Shalai
- **Turn 4-5**: Golos for 5 colorless → fetches The World Tree → now at 6+ lands with World Tree = all colors available
- **Shalai** on turn 4 if you have Plains — now your creatures have hexproof too
- With Sterling Grove + Shalai: your entire board is untargetable

### Turn 5-6: Sanctum of All
- Cast Sanctum of All (WUBRG — World Tree provides all colors)
- Sterling Grove gives it shroud immediately — opponent can't remove it
- From now on: free shrine every upkeep, can't be stopped

### Turn 6+: Untouchable Avalanche
- Sanctum of All fetches Life's Origin → tokens start multiplying
- Every shrine trigger scales: drain, damage, counters, sacrifice, tokens
- Sigarda if needed for anti-sacrifice insurance
- Golos activation (2WUBRG) flips top 3 for even more shrines
- Protection shell means opponent needs a board wipe — and Life's Origin recurs after wipes

## Key Interactions

### Sterling Grove + Shrines
- All shrines are enchantments → all gain shroud from Sterling Grove
- Opponent literally cannot target any shrine with removal
- Sterling Grove itself has shroud (it's an enchantment too) — only board wipes remove it
- Sacrifice Sterling Grove to tutor Sanctum of All if you need to find it

### Shalai + Shrine Creature Tokens
- Go-Shintai of Life's Origin creates 1/1 shrine creature tokens
- Shalai gives them all hexproof — they can't be picked off
- Go-Shintai of Boundless Vigor pumps them with +1/+1 counters
- A board of 6 hexproof shrine tokens getting +6/+6 each per upkeep is lethal fast

### Sigarda Anti-Edict
- Edict effects (Liliana, Sheoldred's Edict, Fleshbag Marauder) bypass hexproof
- Sigarda shuts these down completely — opponents can't make you sacrifice
- She also has hexproof herself + 5/5 flying = hard to deal with independently

### Life's Origin After Board Wipes
- Opponent casts Wrath of God / Doomskar → all creatures die
- Your non-creature shrines (Sanctum of All, Stone Fangs, Fruitful Harvest, Path) survive
- Sanctum of All's next upkeep trigger fetches Life's Origin back from graveyard
- Life's Origin ETB: pay 1 to return another enchantment (shrine) from graveyard
- You rebuild in 1-2 turns while opponent used their whole turn to wipe

### Golos + World Tree + Basics
- Golos costs 5 colorless = any 5 basics cast it
- ETB fetches World Tree → now at 6 lands → World Tree taps for any color
- All 17 basics now produce any color = WUBRG trivially available
- Cultivate on turn 3 → Golos on turn 4 → Sanctum of All on turn 5 is the dream curve

## Matchup Notes

- **Against Aggro**: Cultivate ramps past their curve. Shalai blocks as a 3/4 flyer. Stone Fangs drain keeps your life total up. Hidden Cruelty forces sacrifices. Once protection shell is up, they can't target anything
- **Against Control**: Sterling Grove shroud = their targeted removal is dead. They need Farewell/Wrath specifically. Even after a wipe, Sanctum of All (non-creature, survives creature wipes) fetches everything back. Sigarda dodges edict effects
- **Against Midrange**: You go bigger and they can't interact with your board. Hexproof + shroud means their removal is stranded in hand. Shrine triggers outscale any fair deck
- **Against Combo**: Weakest matchup — you have no counterspells. Race them. Golos on 4-5, Sanctum on 5-6, hope drain from Stone Fangs + damage from Ancient Wars closes before they combo off

## Upgrade Considerations

**Cards to look for:**
- **Dryad of the Ilysian Grove** (2G) — redundant World Tree effect on a body. Extra land drop. You have 2x
- **Idyllic Tutor** (2W) — finds Sanctum of All or Sterling Grove on demand. You have 2x
- **Leyline Binding** (W) — with domain from basics, 1-mana exile removal. Enchantment type = Sythis draws off it
- **Doomskar** (1WW foretell) — 3-mana board wipe. Your non-creature shrines survive
- **Hallowed Haunting** (2WW) — creates flying spirits on every enchantment cast. You have 1x
- **Sanctum of Fruitful Harvest** (2G) — mana generation shrine, fuels Golos activations. Slot in if you want more shrines over protection
- **Go-Shintai of Ancient Wars** (2R) — direct damage shrine, doubles as removal. If aggro isn't a problem, swap for Sphere of Safety
