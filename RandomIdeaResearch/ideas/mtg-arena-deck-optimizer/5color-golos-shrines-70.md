# 5-Color Golos Shrines (70 Cards)
**Format**: Historic / Timeless
**Archetype**: 5-Color Enchantment Combo with Protection Shell
**Win Condition**: Exponential shrine triggers — drain, damage, tokens, +1/+1 counters — protected by hexproof + shroud + board wipe insurance

---

## The Game Plan

```
TURN 1: Commune with Spirits ──→ dig 4 deep for Sythis, Path, Sterling Grove, or a shrine
         Delighted Halfling ──→ mana dork, makes legendary spells uncounterable

TURNS 2-3: Card draw engine + ramp + interaction
  Sythis (GW) ──→ every enchantment draws a card + gains 1 life
  Wolfwillow Haven (1G) ──→ enchantment ramp (Sythis draws! Sterling protects!)
  Path to the World Tree (1G) ──→ enchantment ramp (Sythis draws off this too!)
  Sterling Grove (GW) ──→ shroud on all enchantments
  Leyline Binding (W) ──→ 1-mana exile any nonland (enchantment = Sythis draws!)
  Cultivate (2G) ──→ 2 basics (reliable with 17 basics)
  Doomskar (foretell 1WW) ──→ foretell turn 2, wipe board turn 3

TURN 4-5: Golos + Protection
  Golos (5 colorless) ──→ fetches The World Tree ──→ all colors online
  Shalai (3W) ──→ you + all creatures gain hexproof

TURN 5-6: Sanctum of All (WUBRG)
  Protected by Sterling Grove shroud ──→ opponent can't remove it
  Free shrine every upkeep, can't be stopped

TURN 6+: Untouchable shrine avalanche
  Life's Origin ──→ exponential shrine tokens
  Stone Fangs ──→ drain X life per upkeep
  Hidden Cruelty ──→ force sacrifice per upkeep
  Sphere of Safety ──→ opponents can't attack
  Sythis ──→ every shrine draws + gains life
  Sterling Grove + Shalai + Sigarda ──→ NOTHING can be targeted
```

## Mulligan Rules

### Always Keep
- 2-3 lands (green source) + Commune or Path = you'll find what you need
- Forest + Sythis + any ramp = dream start, drawing cards by turn 3
- Forest + Commune + Cultivate + 2 lands = Commune finds Sythis/Path, Cultivate ramps
- 2 lands + Doomskar + any ramp = foretell turn 1, wipe turn 3, ramp after

### Always Mulligan
- No green source (no Forest, no Fabled Passage)
- No plays before turn 4 (hand of Golos, Sanctum, Life's Origin, Sigarda)
- 5+ lands with no action
- All protection, no shrines or ramp

### On 6 Cards
- Keep any hand with 2 lands + green source + any playable spell
- Keep 3 lands + Commune (it finds whatever you're missing)
- Keep 2 lands + Doomskar + anything (you have time)

### On 5 Cards
- Keep 2 lands + anything. Commune or Sythis off the top saves you

## How It Plays

### Turn 1: Set Up
- **Commune with Spirits**: dig for Sythis (card engine), Path/Wolfwillow (ramp), or Sterling Grove (protection)
- **Delighted Halfling**: mana dork, ramps to turn 2 Cultivate or double 2-drop
- **Foretell Doomskar**: if you smell aggro, hide it face-down for a turn 3 board wipe

### Turns 2-3: Ramp + Draw Engine
- **Sythis turn 2**: now every enchantment draws a card. Path draws. Wolfwillow draws. Shrines draw. Sterling draws. You're churning through your deck
- **Path/Wolfwillow turn 2**: enchantment ramp, protected by Sterling Grove if it's out
- **Leyline Binding**: if opponent plays something scary early, exile it for W
- **Cultivate turn 3**: 2 basics, gets you to 5 lands for Golos
- **Doomskar turn 3**: if you foretold turn 1-2, wipe the board for 1WW. Your enchantments survive

### Turns 4-5: Golos + Shalai
- **Golos for 5 colorless** → fetches World Tree → all colors online
- **Shalai** on turn 4: hexproof on everything
- With Sterling + Shalai: entire board is untargetable

### Turn 5-6: Sanctum of All
- Cast for WUBRG (World Tree provides all colors)
- Sterling Grove = shroud on Sanctum immediately
- Free shrine every upkeep from now on

### Turn 6+: Avalanche
- Sanctum fetches Life's Origin → tokens multiply exponentially
- Every upkeep: drain, counters, forced sacrifice, more tokens
- Sphere of Safety = can't be attacked
- Golos activation (2WUBRG) = play top 3 free

## Key Interactions

### Leyline Binding — The Swiss Army Knife
- With Forest + Plains + Swamp + Island + Mountain in play, domain = 5, cost is just W
- Exiles any nonland permanent at instant speed — creatures, planeswalkers, enchantments, artifacts
- It's an ENCHANTMENT: Sythis draws off it, Sterling Grove gives it shroud (can't be disenchanted!), Sphere counts it
- Idyllic Tutor can find it as removal when needed
- Commune with Spirits can find it too

### Doomskar — The Reset Button
- Foretell face-down for 2 generic mana (turn 1-2)
- Cast for 1WW on turn 3 = board wipe for 3 mana
- Your non-creature enchantments survive: Sanctum of All, Stone Fangs, Path, Sterling Grove, Sphere of Safety, Wolfwillow Haven, Leyline Binding
- After wipe: Sanctum of All upkeep trigger fetches Life's Origin from graveyard → you rebuild instantly, opponent doesn't
- Against aggro: this single card buys you 3-4 turns to set up shrines

### Wolfwillow Haven — Better Than It Looks
- 2-mana enchantment ramp: goes on a Forest, now it taps for extra G
- It's an enchantment: Sythis draws, Sterling protects, Sphere counts
- Late game: sacrifice for a 2/2 wolf token (chump blocker or surprise attacker)
- Commune with Spirits finds it (enchantment)
- Idyllic Tutor finds it (enchantment)
- Faster than Cultivate: turn 2 Wolfwillow → turn 3 you have 4 mana → turn 4 Golos

### Sythis + Everything
- 28 enchantments in deck → Sythis draws off nearly half your spells
- Path draws. Wolfwillow draws. Leyline Binding draws. Sterling draws. Every shrine draws
- Life gain adds up: casting 3-4 enchantments per turn = 3-4 life per turn
- She's a must-kill threat... but Shalai gives her hexproof and Sterling gives your enchantments shroud

### Idyllic Tutor — Find Anything
- Finds Sanctum of All (engine)
- Finds Sterling Grove (protection)
- Finds Sphere of Safety (aggro lockout)
- Finds Leyline Binding (removal)
- Finds Wolfwillow Haven (ramp)
- Finds any shrine
- 2 copies = you'll see one most games

### Life's Origin After Board Wipes
- Creature wipes kill your creatures but NOT enchantments
- Sanctum of All, Stone Fangs, Path, Sterling Grove, Sphere, Wolfwillow, Leyline Binding all survive
- Next upkeep: Sanctum fetches Life's Origin from graveyard
- Life's Origin ETB: pay 1 to return another enchantment from graveyard
- You rebuild in 1-2 turns while opponent has nothing

## Matchup Notes

- **Against Aggro**: Doomskar on turn 3 wipes their board. Leyline Binding exiles their best creature for 1 mana. Sythis gains life. Shalai blocks as 3/4 flyer. Sphere of Safety locks out attacks. Stone Fangs drains. You have answers at every stage now
- **Against Control**: Sterling Grove = shroud on everything, their targeted removal is dead. Delighted Halfling = uncounterable Golos/Sanctum. Leyline Binding has shroud from Sterling (they can't remove your removal). Life's Origin recurs from graveyard after Farewell
- **Against Midrange**: Leyline Binding picks off their best threat. You outscale with exponential shrine triggers. Hexproof + shroud = their removal is stranded. Sphere prevents profitable attacks
- **Against Combo**: Still weakest matchup but better now. Leyline Binding can exile a combo piece at instant speed. Doomskar can disrupt creature-based combos. Race with Golos → Sanctum → drain
