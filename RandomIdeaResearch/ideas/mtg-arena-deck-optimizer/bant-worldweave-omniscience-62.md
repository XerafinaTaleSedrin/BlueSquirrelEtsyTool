# Bant Worldweave Omniscience — Historic BO1 (62 Cards)

**Format**: Historic BO1
**Archetype**: Bant (W/U/G) Worldweave Ramp → Omniscience Combo
**Strategy**: Use Worldweave to seek lands every time you cast a creature, ramping to 10 mana naturally by turn 5-6, then slam Omniscience and cast everything for free — including Ornate Imitations for a massive army of conjured creatures.

---

## The Engine

```
THE RAMP LOOP:

  Worldweave on battlefield (enchantment, 2G)
  → Cast any creature spell
  → Worldweave triggers: seek a land, put it onto battlefield tapped
  → You now have +1 land AND a creature

  With multiple Worldweaves or cheap creatures + blink spells:
  → Turn 3: Worldweave
  → Turn 4: Cast 2-3 cheap creatures = seek 2-3 lands (you're at 7-8 mana)
  → Turn 5: Cast Omniscience (7UUU) → everything is free
  → Cast Ornate Imitations X=10+ → conjure creatures at every mana value
  → Cast Enter the Infinite → draw your entire deck → cast everything free

THE BLINK ENGINE:

  Distinguished Conjurer on battlefield (1W, 1/2)
  → Any creature ETB = gain 1 life
  → Cloudshift/Essence Flux your creature = re-trigger all ETBs
  → Fear of Change ETB: exile your creature, conjure a BIGGER random creature
  → Each blink = more Worldweave land seeks + Conjurer life + new conjured cards
```

## How It Plays

### Turn 1: Setup
Play Novice Inspector (Clue token), Captive Weird, or Opt to filter.

### Turn 2: Value Engine
Drop Distinguished Conjurer or Fear of Change. Sterling Grove if you need to find Worldweave.

### Turn 3: Worldweave
Land + Worldweave. Now every creature you cast = bonus land.

### Turn 4: Ramp Explosion
Cast 2-3 cheap creatures → seek 2-3 lands → you jump from 4 to 7 mana.
Blink a creature with Cloudshift → re-trigger Worldweave (exile + return = creature ETB = seek another land).

### Turn 5-6: Omniscience
You're at 8-10 mana. Slam Omniscience. Everything in hand is now free.
- Cast Ornate Imitations (X still costs X even under Omniscience — X spells work differently)
- Cast any remaining creatures for free → Worldweave keeps seeking lands
- One with the Multiverse as backup if you drew it instead

### Alternative Win: Value Snowball
Even without Omniscience, Worldweave + blink + Fear of Change generates absurd value. Fear of Change keeps upgrading your creatures to higher MV conjured creatures. Note: conjured creatures entering the battlefield do NOT trigger Worldweave (only cast creature spells do). But the blink spells themselves re-trigger ETBs for massive value.

## Key Play Patterns

```
THE RAMP BURST:
  Worldweave + cast 2 cheap creatures + Cloudshift one
  = 3 Worldweave triggers = 3 sought lands in one turn
  Turn 4 → jump to 7 mana

FEAR OF CHANGE UPGRADE CHAIN:
  Fear of Change ETB → exile Novice Inspector (MV 1)
  → Conjure random creature MV 3 onto battlefield
  → That creature ETB triggers Worldweave (seek a land)
  → Cloudshift Fear of Change → re-trigger
  → Exile the MV 3 → conjure random creature MV 5
  → Another Worldweave trigger

STERLING GROVE TUTOR LINE:
  Turn 2: Sterling Grove (protects your enchantments)
  Turn 3: Sacrifice Sterling Grove → search for Worldweave → cast it
  OR
  Late game: Sacrifice → search for Omniscience → slam it next turn

TEFERI PROTECTION:
  Teferi on board = opponents can't counter your Omniscience
  Cast Omniscience with confidence on your turn
```

## Important Rules Notes

```
ORNATE IMITATIONS + OMNISCIENCE:
  Omniscience says "without paying its mana cost"
  Ornate Imitations costs XGU — X is part of the mana cost
  When cast free via Omniscience, X = 0 → conjures NOTHING
  ⚠️ You must hardcast Ornate Imitations with actual mana for X > 0
  With 10+ lands from Worldweave, cast it for X=8+ legitimately

WORLDWEAVE + BLINK:
  Cloudshift exiles then returns → the creature "enters the battlefield"
  This IS a creature entering → Worldweave triggers → seek a land
  The creature doesn't need to be "cast" — just needs to enter

WORLDWEAVE + CONJURED CREATURES:
  Fear of Change conjures creatures ONTO THE BATTLEFIELD
  Conjured creatures entering = Worldweave triggers
  Each Fear of Change blink = 2 Worldweave triggers (the conjured creature + the returning Fear)
```
