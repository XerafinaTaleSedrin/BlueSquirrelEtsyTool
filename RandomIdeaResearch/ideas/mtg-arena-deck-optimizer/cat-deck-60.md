## The Lord Stack (Why This Wins)

With ALL lords on the field, every cat gets:
- **King of the Pride**: +2/+1
- **Arahbo, the First Fang**: +1/+1
- **Feline Sovereign**: +1/+1
- **Regal Caracal**: +1/+1 AND lifelink
- **Kaheera** (companion): +1/+1 AND vigilance

**Total: +6/+5, lifelink, vigilance**

A 1/1 Sacred Cat becomes a **7/6 lifelink vigilance** creature. Leonin Vanguard becomes **7/6** and gains you 1 life on attack. And they ALL have lifelink from Regal Caracal, so every point of damage = life gained.

## How It Plays

### Turns 1-2: Deploy the Kittens
- Sacred Cat, Leonin Vanguard, Savannah Lions — cheap bodies that apply early pressure
- Ajani's Pridemate starts growing from any lifegain trigger

### Turn 3: Lords Come Online
- Kaheera from companion (always available) OR King of the Pride
- Suddenly your 1/1s are 3/2s or bigger. Start swinging hard
- Arahbo creates free 1/1 cat tokens whenever nontoken cats enter

### Turns 4-5: Overwhelm
- Leonin Warleader attacks = two more cat tokens, tapped and attacking
- Feline Sovereign starts destroying their artifacts and enchantments
- Enduring Curiosity = every cat that connects draws a card. 4 cats hitting = draw 4
- Kutzil shuts down their instants during your turn

### Turn 6+: Crush
- Regal Caracal: lord + lifelink + two tokens
- Ajani -3: give a lord-buffed cat flying and double strike for lethal
- Cathars' Crusade: every new token gives everything +1/+1
- Sovereign Okinec Ahau dumps +1/+1 counters everywhere on attack

## Why This Is Mean

1. **Feline Sovereign eats their stuff.** Every cat that connects destroys an artifact or enchantment. Four cats hit? Four permanents gone. Enchantment decks literally cannot function
2. **Kutzil shuts down their tricks.** No instants on your turn. No combat tricks, no removal in response, nothing. Your turn, your rules
3. **The lord stack is absurd.** Two lords turn Sacred Cat into a 4/3 lifelink. Three lords and it's a 5/4. The math gets disgusting
4. **Nothing stays dead.** Sacred Cat embalms. Enduring Curiosity returns as an enchantment. Lion Sash reconfigures. Your cats have nine lives
5. **Ajani's -3 is lethal.** Give a lord-buffed cat flying and double strike. A 5/4 with double strike = 10 damage in the air. Surprise kill

## Companion Rules Reminder

Kaheera requires **every creature card in your starting deck** to be a Cat, Elemental, Nightmare, Dinosaur, or Beast. Every creature in this deck is a Cat. The only non-creature spells (Claws Out, Three Tree Battalion, Felidar Retreat, Cathars' Crusade, Ajani) don't break the restriction.

**Note on Enduring Curiosity**: It's a Cat Glimmer. "Glimmer" is NOT on Kaheera's allowed list. However, Enduring Curiosity is an Enchantment Creature, and its creature type is "Cat Glimmer" — since it IS a Cat, it satisfies the companion requirement. If Arena flags it, swap for more Prideful Parents or Helpful Hunters.

Enjoy your cat army!
