# Conjuration Game - Optimized 60-Card Build

**Format**: Historic
**Archetype**: Bant (G/W/U) Conjure & Cheat
**Strategy**: Draw tons of cards, conjure random stuff with Worldweave and Fear of Change, then drop Omniscience and cast everything for free. Pure chaos, but focused chaos.

## How It Plays

### Early Game (Turns 1-3)
- **Turn 1**: Novice Inspector — 1/1 body + Clue token
- **Turn 2**: Fear of Change — conjure a random creature to hand! Or Clay-Fired Bricks for ramp
- **Turn 3**: Worldweave — NOW every card you draw conjures something random. The engine is online

### Mid Game (Turns 4-6)
- Crack Clues with Forensic Gadgeteer discount (draw cards → Worldweave triggers → conjured cards pile up)
- Thoughtcast for 1-2 mana draws 2 more cards → 2 more Worldweave triggers
- Alaundo starts suspending expensive cards from hand for future free casting
- You're building a hand FULL of random conjured cards

### Late Game (Turn 7+)
- **Drop Omniscience or One with the Multiverse**
- Cast your entire hand for FREE
- Each spell potentially draws more cards → more Worldweave conjures → more free spells
- **Aetherflux Reservoir** counts each spell cast this turn. Cast 10 spells = gain 55 life. Pay 50 life = opponent dies
- Or just overwhelm with a board full of random conjured creatures

### The Dream Turn
With Omniscience + Worldweave + Aetherflux Reservoir:
1. Cast every card in hand for free (each one triggers Worldweave)
2. Conjured cards go to hand → cast those for free too
3. Aetherflux counts: spell 1 = +1 life, spell 2 = +2, spell 3 = +3... spell 10 = +10 = 55 total life gained
4. Pay 50 life → Aetherflux Reservoir deals 50 damage to opponent
5. They're dead. From random cards. Beautiful.

## What Got Cut (and Why)

### Removed: 5-color cards
- Jodah, Invasion of Alara, Villainous Wealth, Golos
- **Why**: Fun but impossible to cast reliably in 60 cards. Bant (GWU) is the sweet spot for this strategy

### Removed: Expensive do-nothings
- Cosmos Elixir, Tamiyo's Logbook, Mysterious Tome, Crucible of Worlds
- **Why**: Too slow. These are "value over 10 turns" cards. You want to be casting Omniscience by turn 7-8, not fiddling with slow artifacts

### Removed: Off-theme cards
- Soul Warden, Authority of the Consuls, Break the Spell, Light of Hope
- **Why**: Lifegain/removal that doesn't advance the conjuration gameplan. The best defense is casting Omniscience and winning

### Removed: Redundant draw
- Tamiyo's Epiphany (4 mana for 2 cards is too slow when Thoughtcast costs 1-2), Gaea's Blessing (anti-mill, not needed)
- **Why**: Deduce + Thoughtcast + Clues give you enough draw that's cheaper and synergizes with artifacts

### Removed: Win-more cards
- Extravagant Replication, The Great Henge, Smothering Tithe, Tome of the Infinite
- **Why**: Amazing when you're already winning, but don't help you GET to the winning position. Omniscience is the only big payoff you need

## Upgrade Path

If you have wildcards to spare:
- **Growth Spiral** (replaces some They Went This Way) — ramp + draw in one card, instant speed
- **Teferi, Hero of Dominaria** — draw + untap lands, helps reach Omniscience faster
- **Shark Typhoon** — cycling draws a card (Worldweave trigger) AND makes a flying shark
- **Omniscience from M19/M20** — if you need more copies
- **Enter the Infinite** — with Omniscience, draw your entire deck, conjure everything, Aetherflux for the kill
