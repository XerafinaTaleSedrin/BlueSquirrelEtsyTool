# Tome of Power Nine — Izzet Spellbook Storm (Historic Bo1)

**Format:** Historic | **Cards:** 60 | **Colors:** UR
**Archetype:** Spellbook Storm / Chaos Spellslinger
**Status:** Built from confirmed collection — zero wildcards needed

## Why This Deck

Oracle of the Alpha conjures actual Power Nine into your deck. Tome of the Infinite conjures random spells from its spellbook. Thousand-Year Storm copies everything. You're literally casting Black Lotuses and Ancestral Recalls. Every game plays completely differently because you're casting cards that aren't even in your deck.

## The Engine

```
SETUP:
  Oracle of the Alpha ETB → shuffles Power Nine into your library
  Tome of the Infinite → tap to conjure a random spell from its spellbook
  Jalum Tome → tap to draw/discard, churns through deck to find Power Nine

STORM TURN:
  Thousand-Year Storm on battlefield
  → Cast cheap spell #1 (Opt) → 1 copy
  → Cast cheap spell #2 (Shock) → 2 copies
  → Cast cheap spell #3 (Crash Through) → 3 copies
  → Storm-Kiln Artist makes a treasure for EACH cast + copy
  → Balmor gives your team +1/+0 and trample for each instant/sorcery
  → Niv-Mizzet pings for every card drawn

CHAOS CREATURES:
  Chaos Balor → attacks: random effect (sac artifact/enchant, discard, damage)
              → dies: random effect again
  Chaos Spewer → random damage/disruption
  Sower of Chaos → more random mayhem
```

## How It Plays

### Early Game (Turns 1-3)
- Oracle of the Alpha on turn 3 seeds Power Nine into your library
- Jalum Tome / Tome of the Infinite provide card flow and random conjures
- April, Reporter of the Weird conjures random spells
- Cheap spells (Opt, Shock) control the board

### Mid Game (Turns 4-5)
- Storm-Kiln Artist starts printing treasure off every spell
- Balmor turns your creatures into threats with each spell cast
- Chaos creatures create random board disruption
- You might draw a Black Lotus or Ancestral Recall from Oracle's shuffle

### Storm Turn (Turn 6+)
- Thousand-Year Storm lands
- Chain cheap spells: each successive spell gets more copies
- Storm-Kiln makes treasure for each cast AND each copy
- Niv-Mizzet pings for every card drawn
- Balmor pumps the whole team with each spell
- Cast whatever random Power Nine / Tome conjures you've accumulated

### The Dream Sequence

```
Board: Thousand-Year Storm + Storm-Kiln Artist + Niv-Mizzet

Cast Opt (1st spell) → 1 copy → draw 2, scry 2, 2 treasures, 2 pings
Cast Crash Through (2nd) → 2 copies → draw 3, trample x3, 3 treasures, 3 pings
Cast Shock (3rd) → 3 copies → 8 damage to face, 4 treasures, Niv pinging
Cast Ancestral Recall from Oracle (4th) → 4 copies → draw 15 cards, 5 treasures
    → Niv-Mizzet pings 15 times → game over
```
