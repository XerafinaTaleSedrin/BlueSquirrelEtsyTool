# Mono-Black Drain — Standard Pauper (Midweek Magic)

**Format**: Standard Pauper (commons + basic lands only)
**Archetype**: Mono-Black Aggro/Drain
**Strategy**: Deploy cheap evasive creatures, drain life with attack triggers and death triggers, remove blockers efficiently, and grind opponent down with incremental life loss. Every creature either drains, has evasion, or replaces itself on death.

## How It Plays

### Turn 1: Start the Clock
- **Pulse Tracker** — attacks for 1 damage + 1 drain = 2 life swing per attack
- **Vampire Neonate** — blocks their 1-drop, taps to drain every turn
- **Duress** — strip their removal or combat trick before they can use it

### Turn 2: Build Pressure
- **Sanguine Syphoner** — 1/3 lifelink attacker, tough to trade with
- **Barrow Naughty** — flying damage they can't block
- **Dead Weight** — kill their early creature

### Turn 3: The Payoff
- **Marauding Blight-Priest** — NOW every lifelink hit and Neonate tap drains DOUBLE
  - Sanguine Syphoner attacks with lifelink → gain 1 life → Blight-Priest drains 1 more
  - Vampire Neonate tap → gain 1 life → Blight-Priest drains 1 more
  - Moment of Craving → gain 2 life → Blight-Priest drains 2 more

### Turn 4+: Grind Them Out
- Multiple drain sources ticking every turn
- Removal keeps their board clear
- Evasive creatures (flying, menace) push damage through
- They're losing 3-5 life per turn from drain effects alone

## Synergy Map

```
Vampire Neonate ──→ tap: opponent loses 1, you gain 1
Sanguine Syphoner ──→ attacks with lifelink → you gain life
Barrow Naughty ──→ attacks with lifelink → you gain life
Moment of Craving ──→ you gain 2 life
        │
        ▼
ALL lifegain triggers Marauding Blight-Priest
        │
        ▼
Opponent loses 1 life for EACH instance of lifegain
        │
        ▼
Combined: Neonate tap + Syphoner attack + Blight-Priest = 4+ life swing per turn

Pulse Tracker ──→ attacks = opponent loses 1 (no lifegain needed, just aggro)
Fear of Lost Teeth ──→ dies = 1 damage + 1 life (triggers Blight-Priest too!)
Acolyte of Aclazotz ──→ tap: drain 1 (triggers Blight-Priest!)

Duress/Pilfer ──→ strip their answers before they can use them
Dead Weight/Stab/Moment/Murder ──→ clear blockers so your drainers get through
```

---

## Win Conditions

1. **Blight-Priest drain engine** — Every lifelink creature and tap drain triggers extra life loss. Multiple Blight-Priests = multiple triggers per lifegain
2. **Evasive beatdown** — Flying and menace creatures push past blockers
3. **Attrition** — Your creatures gain life, theirs don't. You grind them out over 6-8 turns
4. **Hand disruption** — Duress and Pilfer remove their best cards. They can't answer what they don't have

---

## Tips for Pauper

- **Mulligan for**: At least one 1-drop creature + a 2-drop. Blight-Priest in opening hand is ideal but not required
- **Don't be afraid to trade** — your creatures have death triggers and you have plenty of threats
- **Save Moment of Craving for when Blight-Priest is out** if possible — it's 4 life swing instead of 2
- **Tap Vampire Neonate on opponent's end step** — drain them before your turn, keep mana open for removal
