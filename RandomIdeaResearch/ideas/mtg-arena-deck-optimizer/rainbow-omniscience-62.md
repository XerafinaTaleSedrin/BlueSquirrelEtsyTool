# Rainbow Omniscience — Historic BO1 (62 Cards)

**Format**: Historic BO1
**Archetype**: 5-Color Worldweave Ramp → Omniscience Combo
**Strategy**: Use Worldweave to seek lands every time you cast a creature, ramping to Omniscience by turn 5-6. Blink creatures to re-trigger ETBs. Yarok and Elesh Norn Mother of Machines DOUBLE all ETB triggers. Terror of the Peaks turns every ETB into direct damage.

---

## The Engine

```
THE RAMP LOOP:

  Worldweave on battlefield (enchantment, 2G)
  → Cast any creature spell
  → Worldweave triggers: seek a land, put it onto battlefield tapped
  → Lotus Cobra: each land entering = 1 mana of any color
  → Net result: every creature you cast GENERATES mana

  With Lotus Cobra + Worldweave:
  → Turn 3: Worldweave
  → Turn 4: Cast Lotus Cobra + 2 cheap creatures
  → Each creature = Worldweave seeks land = Lotus Cobra makes mana
  → You're at 8+ mana by turn 4

THE ETB DOUBLER ENGINE:

  Elesh Norn, Mother of Machines OR Yarok, the Desecrated
  → ALL ETB triggers happen TWICE
  → Distinguished Conjurer: gain 2 life per creature ETB
  → Terror of the Peaks: deal DOUBLE damage per creature ETB
  → Fear of Change: exile + conjure TWICE (two random creatures!)
  → Worldweave: seek TWO lands per creature cast
  → With BOTH doublers: TRIPLE all ETBs

THE BLINK ENGINE:

  Thassa, Deep-Dwelling: free blink every end step
  Soulherder: free blink every end step + grows
  Cloudshift/Essence Flux: instant-speed re-trigger all ETBs
  Yorion: mass blink everything on ETB
```

## How It Plays

### Turn 1: Setup
Novice Inspector (Clue token) or hold up Cloudshift.

### Turn 2: Value Engine
Distinguished Conjurer or Fear of Change. Sterling Grove if you need to find Worldweave.

### Turn 3: Worldweave
Land + Worldweave. Every creature from here = bonus land.

### Turn 4: Ramp Explosion
Cast Lotus Cobra + cheap creatures. Each creature → Worldweave seeks land → Lotus Cobra makes mana → you net mana on every creature. Jump from 4 mana to 8-9.

### Turn 5-6: Omniscience
Slam Omniscience. Everything in hand is free. Chain:
- Terror of the Peaks (free) → now every creature ETB deals damage
- Koma (free, uncounterable) → 6/6 body, Terror deals 6 damage
- Yorion (free) → blink everything → all ETBs re-trigger → Terror deals damage for each
- Hullbreaker Horror (free) → every subsequent spell bounces something

### Alternative Win: Value Snowball
Even without Omniscience, the ETB doubler engine (Elesh Norn MoM + Yarok + Terror of the Peaks + Thassa blink every turn) generates overwhelming value. Each blink = 2-3x damage from Terror + life from Conjurer + land from Worldweave.

## Key Synergy Combos

```
LOTUS COBRA + WORLDWEAVE (turns 3-4):
  Cast creature → Worldweave seeks land → land enters →
  Lotus Cobra: +1 mana any color
  Net result: creatures cost 1 less (or are mana-positive with multiple Cobras)

ELESH NORN MOM + TERROR OF THE PEAKS:
  Any creature ETB → Terror triggers TWICE (doubled by Elesh Norn)
  A 4-power creature entering = 8 damage to any target
  Yorion mass-blink → EVERY creature re-enters → double Terror damage for each

YAROK + ELESH NORN MOM (TRIPLE ETBs):
  Both on field = all ETBs trigger THREE times
  Terror of the Peaks: creature enters → deal power damage x3
  Distinguished Conjurer: gain 3 life per creature
  Worldweave: seek 3 lands per creature cast

THASSA + ANY POWER CREATURE (every end step):
  Thassa blinks Terror of the Peaks → Terror re-enters → deals 5 to any target
  Thassa blinks Yorion → Yorion re-enters → mass blink everything → chain reaction
  Thassa blinks Titan of Industry → choose 2 modes again

HULLBREAKER + OMNISCIENCE (lockout):
  Every free spell bounces a permanent or spell
  Cast creature (free) → bounce their best permanent
  Cast another → bounce another
  Opponents can never keep anything on board

STERLING GROVE TUTOR LINES:
  Sacrifice → find Worldweave (turn 3 setup)
  Sacrifice → find Omniscience (turn 5-6 when you have mana)
  While on field: Worldweave and Omniscience have shroud
```
