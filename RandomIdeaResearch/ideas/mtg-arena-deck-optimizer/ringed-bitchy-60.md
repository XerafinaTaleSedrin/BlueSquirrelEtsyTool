# Ringed Bitchy Deck - Optimized 60-Card Build

**Format**: Historic
**Archetype**: Mono-Black Drain & Punishment
**Strategy**: Drain your opponent's life while punishing them for drawing cards, then close with Vito + Exquisite Blood infinite combo, Gray Merchant devotion bomb, or Nazgul beatdown.

---

## What Changed (Luxury Upgrades - 2026-03-07)

Incorporated 8 owned upgrades (all 1-ofs) to raise the deck's power ceiling while keeping the core drain engine intact.

**Cards Added:**
- **Fatal Push** (1x) — Replaced 1x Cut Down. Kills more threats, revolt is easy with sacrifice effects
- **Gifted Aetherborn** (1x) — 2/3 deathtouch lifelink for BB. Blocks anything, lifelink triggers Vito/Blight-Priest
- **Gray Merchant of Asphodel** (1x) — Drains equal to your black devotion. With Sheoldred (BB), Ayara (BBB), Nazgul (B), the drain is massive. Bloodletter DOUBLES it
- **Spawn of Mayhem** (1x) — 4/4 flying trample for 2BB (spectacle = 2B). Drains 1 every upkeep automatically
- **Rankle, Master of Pranks** (1x) — 3/3 flying haste. On combat damage: everyone discards, sacrifices, or draws+loses 1 life. All three modes synergize with drain
- **Murderous Rider // Swift End** (1x) — Adventure: destroy any creature or planeswalker for 1BB (you lose 2 life). Then cast the 2/3 lifelink body. Premium removal + threat in one card
- **Phyrexian Arena** (1x) — Draw an extra card every upkeep, lose 1 life. The card advantage is enormous and the life loss is negligible with all your lifegain
- **Castle Locthwain** (1x) — Replaced 1x Swamp. Draw a card for BB + life loss. Free upgrade since it enters untapped with a Swamp

**Cards Cut:**
- 1x Cut Down (Fatal Push is better)
- 1x Elspeth's Nightmare (Murderous Rider is superior removal)
- 1x Marauding Blight-Priest (2 to 1; enough drain multipliers now)
- 1x Tinybones Joins Up (2 to 1; less discard focus with better creatures)
- 1x Knight of the Ebon Legion (weakest card, replaced by better threats)
- 1x Duress (Thoughtseize already covers this, and we have better cards now)
- 1x Howling Mine (2 to 1; risky card that helps opponent)
- 1x Swamp (Castle Locthwain replaces it)

## How It Plays

### Early Game (Turns 1-3)
- **Turn 1**: Thoughtseize to strip their best card, or Fatal Push/Cut Down to answer their 1-drop
- **Turn 2**: Deep-Cavern Bat — see their hand, exile their best nonland card, AND get a 1/1 flyer. Or Gifted Aetherborn as a 2/3 deathtouch lifelink wall. Or Sanctum of Stone Fangs for drain
- **Turn 3**: Nazgul (ring tempts you), Vito/Blight-Priest (drain multipliers), Phyrexian Arena (card engine), or Murderous Rider adventure side to destroy a threat

### Mid Game (Turns 4-5)
- **Turn 4**: SHEOLDRED. Now every card they draw costs 2 life. Every card you draw gains 2. Or Spawn of Mayhem as a 4/4 flying trample that drains every upkeep. Or Rankle to fly in with haste and force discards/sacrifices
- **Turn 5**: Gray Merchant of Asphodel to drain for your entire black devotion (often 6-10+ damage). Bloodletter DOUBLES this. Or Exquisite Blood to set up the infinite combo
- Meanwhile: Keep removing their threats with Fatal Push, Cut Down, Go for the Throat, and Swift End

### Late Game (Win Conditions)
1. **Sheoldred + Howling Mine grind**: They lose 4+ life per turn just from drawing. With Sanctum, Blight-Priest, Spawn of Mayhem, and Vito? Even more
2. **Vito + Exquisite Blood combo**: Any instance of lifegain triggers Vito (opponent loses life) → triggers Exquisite Blood (you gain life) → triggers Vito → infinite loop. GAME OVER
3. **Gray Merchant devotion bomb**: With Sheoldred (BB) + Ayara (BBB) + Nazgul (B) + Gary (BB) on board, Gary drains for 8+. Bloodletter doubles it to 16+. Often lethal on the spot
4. **Nazgul beatdown**: 4+ Nazgul on board buffing each other while the ring tempts you
5. **Evasive pressure**: Rankle (flying haste), Spawn of Mayhem (flying trample), and Bloodletter (flying lifelink) apply constant aerial pressure while draining

## What Got Cut (and Why)

### Removed: Off-color cards
- Nicol Bolas, Sauron, Golos, Kaya, Ugin, Frodo, Smeagol, Gaea's Blessing, Fracture
- **Why**: 5-color mana in 249 cards = you never had the right lands. Mono-black means every land works every time

### Removed: High-cost cards that are too slow
- Rise of the Dark Realms (9 mana), Painful Quandary (5 mana), Tergrid (5 mana), Aclazotz (5 mana)
- **Why**: In a 60-card deck with a low curve, you want to kill them before turn 7, not tap out for one big thing

### Removed: Redundant removal
- Had 32 instants and 39 sorceries. Trimmed to the 12 most efficient
- **Why**: You only need enough removal to survive, not a deck that's 50% removal

### Removed: Shrine/Leyline package
- Honden, Go-Shintai, Leyline of Sanctity, Leyline of the Void, etc.
- **Why**: Shrines need 5 colors and multiple shrines to work. In 60 cards mono-black, Sanctum of Stone Fangs alone is better than a fragmented shrine package

### Removed: Situational/weak cards
- Warlock Class, Torment of Scarabs, Wasteland Scorpion, Leech Gauntlet, etc.
- **Why**: These are "mean" in concept but too slow or low-impact for competitive play
