**Format**: Historic
**Archetype**: Mono-Black Drain & Punishment

## How It Plays

### Early Game (Turns 1-3)
- **Turn 1**: Duress to strip their best card, or Knight of the Ebon Legion as a clock
- **Turn 2**: Deep-Cavern Bat — see their hand, exile their best nonland card, AND get a 1/1 flyer. Or Sanctum of Stone Fangs for drain
- **Turn 3**: Nazgul (ring tempts you) or Vito/Blight-Priest (drain multipliers)

### Mid Game (Turns 4-5)
- **Turn 4**: SHEOLDRED. Now every card they draw costs 2 life. Every card you draw gains 2
- **Turn 5**: Howling Mine — now they draw 2 per turn, losing 4 life per turn to Sheoldred. You draw 2, gaining 4. The life totals diverge FAST
- Meanwhile: Keep removing their threats with Cut Down and Go for the Throat

### Late Game (Win Conditions)
1. **Sheoldred + Howling Mine grind**: They lose 4+ life per turn just from drawing. With Sanctum, Blight-Priest, and Vito? Even more
2. **Vito + Exquisite Blood combo**: Any instance of lifegain triggers Vito (opponent loses life) → triggers Exquisite Blood (you gain life) → triggers Vito → infinite loop. GAME OVER
3. **Nazgul beatdown**: 4+ Nazgul on board buffing each other while the ring tempts you

## What Got Cut (and Why)

### Removed: Off-color cards
- Nicol Bolas, Sauron, Golos, Kaya, Ugin, Frodo, Smeagol, Gaea's Blessing, Fracture
- **Why**: 5-color mana in 249 cards = you never had the right lands. Mono-black means every land works every time

### Removed: High-cost cards that are too slow
- Rise of the Dark Realms (9 mana), Painful Quandary (5 mana), Tergrid (5 mana), Aclazotz (5 mana)
- **Why**: In a 60-card deck with a low curve, you want to kill them before turn 7, not tap out for one big thing

### Removed: Redundant removal
- Had 32 instants and 39 sorceries. Trimmed to the 12 most efficient
- **Why**: You only need enough removal to survive, not a deck that's 50% removal

### Removed: Shrine/Leyline package
- Honden, Go-Shintai, Leyline of Sanctity, Leyline of the Void, etc.
- **Why**: Shrines need 5 colors and multiple shrines to work. In 60 cards mono-black, Sanctum of Stone Fangs alone is better than a fragmented shrine package

### Removed: Situational/weak cards
- Warlock Class, Torment of Scarabs, Wasteland Scorpion, Leech Gauntlet, etc.
- **Why**: These are "mean" in concept but too slow or low-impact for competitive play
