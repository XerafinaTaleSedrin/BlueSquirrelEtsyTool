# Selesnya Enchantress — Optimized 60-Card Build

**Format**: Historic
**Archetype**: Green-White Enchantress Aggro-Combo
**Strategy**: Flood the board with cheap enchantments and enchantment creatures. Sythis draws a card for every enchantment cast. Jukai Naturalist makes them all cost 1 less. Ethereal Armor turns any creature into a massive first-striking monster. Generous Visitor puts +1/+1 counters on your creatures with every enchantment cast. Sterling Grove protects your entire board and tutors for any enchantment. If they stabilize? Heliod + Soul Warden/Ajani's Welcome + Scurry Oak goes infinite — unlimited squirrels, unlimited life. Generous Visitor adds ANOTHER infinite line with Scurry Oak.

**Built from**: Your card collection (1097 cards from MTGA sync)

## The Core Engine

This deck runs on three interlocking systems:

```
DRAW ENGINE:
  Cast enchantment → Sythis draws a card + gains 1 life
                   → Jukai Naturalist made it cost 1 less
                   → Generous Visitor puts a +1/+1 counter on a creature
                   → Cast ANOTHER enchantment...
                                    ↓
VOLTRON KILL:
  Ethereal Armor counts every enchantment you control
  4 enchantments on board = +5/+5 first strike
  7 enchantments on board = +8/+8 first strike
  Put it on Jukai Naturalist = 10/10 first strike on turn 4
                                    ↓
INFINITE COMBO — THREE PATHS:

  Path 1: Heliod + Soul Warden/Ajani's Welcome + Scurry Oak
  → Scurry Oak enters, Soul Warden gains 1 life
  → Heliod puts a +1/+1 counter on Scurry Oak
  → Scurry Oak makes a 1/1 Squirrel token
  → Soul Warden gains 1 life from the Squirrel entering
  → Heliod puts another +1/+1 counter → INFINITE LOOP

  Path 2: Generous Visitor + Scurry Oak + any lifegain (Soul Warden/Ajani's Welcome)
  → Cast any enchantment → Visitor puts counter on Scurry Oak
  → Scurry Oak makes a Squirrel → Soul Warden gains life
  → Heliod puts counter on Oak → another Squirrel → INFINITE

  Path 3: Generous Visitor + Scurry Oak + Sythis
  → Cast enchantment → Visitor puts counter on Oak → Squirrel
  → Sythis gains 1 life → Heliod puts counter → INFINITE

  = More ways to go infinite = more consistent combo kills
```

## How It Plays

### Turn 1: Drop an Enchantment
- **Soul Warden** — starts gaining life immediately
- **Generous Visitor** — enchantment creature, will grow your board with every future enchantment
- **Ajani's Welcome** — lifegain enchantment, ready for creatures
- **Authority of the Consuls** — opponent's creatures enter tapped
- **Sigarda's Aid** — sets up instant-speed Ethereal Armor for later

### Turn 2: The Engine Starts
- **Sythis** — now every enchantment draws a card
- **Jukai Naturalist** — now enchantments cost 1 less
- With Jukai out: cast Ethereal Armor for FREE, Ajani's Welcome for FREE, Authority for FREE
- Sythis draws a card for each one. You just drew 2-3 cards on turn 2

### Turn 3: Explosive
- With Sythis + Jukai on board, cast 3-4 enchantments in one turn
- Each one draws a card (Sythis) and costs 1 less (Jukai)
- **Ethereal Armor** on Jukai with 5 enchantments = 8/8 first strike. Swing for 8
- OR drop **Scurry Oak** to set up combo next turn

### Turn 4-5: Win the Game
- **Plan A — Voltron**: Ethereal Armor on a creature with 6-8 enchantments on board = 9/9 first strike or bigger. Two swings = dead
- **Plan B — Infinite Combo**: Heliod + Soul Warden + Scurry Oak all on board? Scurry Oak gets a +1/+1 counter (from evolve, Heliod trigger, anything). Makes a Squirrel. Soul Warden gains life. Heliod puts another counter. Another Squirrel. Repeat infinitely. Attack with a million squirrels next turn
- **Plan C — Card Advantage**: Sythis has drawn you 8+ cards. You have more threats than they have answers. Just keep playing enchantments and attacking

## Win Conditions

1. **Ethereal Armor voltron** — +1/+1 and first strike per enchantment you control. With 5-8 enchantments, any creature becomes a 2-3 turn clock. The primary win condition
2. **Heliod + Soul Warden/Ajani's Welcome + Scurry Oak infinite** — unlimited squirrels + unlimited life. Classic combo, now in a tighter 60-card shell
3. **Generous Visitor + Scurry Oak + any lifegain** — cast any enchantment, Visitor puts counter on Oak, Oak makes Squirrel, lifegain triggers Heliod, more counters = infinite. A SECOND combo line that's easier to assemble

5. **Enchantment creature beatdown** — Jukai, Destiny Spinner, Sythis, Generous Visitor growing with every spell. Sterling Grove protects them all

## Why Not Shrines?

You have 19 shrine cards, but shrines want **5 colors** to reach their potential. Go-Shintai of Life's Origin counts ALL shrines, and the payoff scales with shrine count across colors. In a focused GW deck, you'd only have 5-6 different shrines — not enough for the shrine count to matter.

A dedicated 5-color Shrine deck is a separate build. For this deck, the enchantress engine (Sythis + Jukai + Ethereal Armor) is far more powerful than a half-powered shrine package.
