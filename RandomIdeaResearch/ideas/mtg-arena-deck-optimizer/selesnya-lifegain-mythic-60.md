# Selesnya Lifegain Combo — Mythic Climb Build (60 Cards)

**Format**: Historic BO1
**Archetype**: Selesnya (G/W) Lifegain + Infinite Combo
**Strategy**: Flood the board with lifegain triggers, grow creatures to lethal in 2-3 turns, or assemble the Heliod + Scurry Oak + Soul Warden infinite combo for an instant win.
**Wildcards needed**: ZERO — built entirely from confirmed owned cards.

---

## The Infinite Combo

```
THE LOOP (instant win):

  Soul Warden on battlefield (or Lunarch Veteran or Ajani's Welcome)
  + Heliod, Sun-Crowned on battlefield
  + Scurry Oak on battlefield

  1. Any creature enters → Soul Warden triggers → gain 1 life
  2. Heliod sees lifegain → puts +1/+1 counter on Scurry Oak
  3. Scurry Oak gets counter → creates 1/1 Squirrel token
  4. Squirrel enters → Soul Warden triggers → gain 1 life
  → BACK TO STEP 2. REPEAT INFINITELY.

  RESULT: Infinite Squirrel tokens + infinite life
  NEXT TURN: Attack with infinite Squirrels. Game over.

  With Conclave Mentor also on board:
  → Each Heliod counter becomes TWO counters on Scurry Oak
  → Scurry Oak triggers TWICE per loop iteration
  → Squirrels generate even faster (not that you need them to)
```

## Blossoming Sands Note

Yes, it enters tapped. In most decks that's bad. In THIS deck, the 1 life trigger is genuine value — it grows Trelasarra, Hallowed Priest, and Voice of the Blessed on the turn it enters. It's not ideal compared to an untapped dual, but it's not dead weight either. Think of it as a dual land that also casts a free spell.

## How It Plays

### Turn 1: Lifegain Trigger
Play Soul Warden, Lunarch Veteran, or Ajani's Welcome.
**Priority**: Soul Warden > Ajani's Welcome > Lunarch Veteran

### Turn 2: Payoff Creature
Drop Trelasarra, Hallowed Priest, Conclave Mentor, or Voice of the Blessed. They immediately start growing.

### Turn 3: Combo or Pressure
- **Have Heliod + Soul Warden + Scurry Oak?** → combo is live. Infinite.
- **No combo?** → Play Scurry Oak. Evolve triggers make Squirrels. Value snowball.
- **Multiple payoffs growing?** → Just attack. 5/5+ creatures on turn 3-4.

### Turn 4+: Win
- **Combo**: infinite Squirrels → swing next turn
- **Beatdown**: 2-drops are 5/5+ by now. Voice has flying at 4 counters.
- **Grind**: Lunarch Veteran returns from graveyard. Engine keeps triggering.

## Key Play Patterns

```
THE SNOWBALL (every game):
  Any lifegain trigger + any payoff = growing threat every turn
  Turn 1 Soul Warden → Turn 2 Trelasarra → Turn 3 anything
  = Trelasarra is already 4/4+ with scry filtering your draws

THE INFINITE COMBO (game-ending):
  Soul Warden + Heliod + Scurry Oak = infinite tokens + life
  → Point Heliod's counter at Scurry Oak
  → Each Squirrel triggers Soul Warden → Heliod adds counter → repeat

THE DOUBLE DIP (maximum value):
  Multiple lifegain triggers + Conclave Mentor
  → Every creature = 2+ triggers, each counter doubled
  → Creatures grow at 4x normal rate

PROTECTION TIMING:
  Hold G open when combo pieces are in danger
  Blossoming Defense for hexproof when they target removal at your key pieces
```

## Matchups

| Matchup | Plan |
|---------|------|
| **Aggro** | Lifegain outraces damage. Soul Warden neutralizes their math. Block with growing creatures |
| **Control** | Deploy faster than they can answer. Ajani's Welcome dodges creature removal. Combo threatens instant win if they tap out |
| **Midrange** | You grow 2-3 creatures simultaneously. They can't block 5/5+ 2-drops profitably |
| **Mirror** | Soul Warden triggers on THEIR creatures too. First to Heliod + Scurry Oak wins |

## Mulligan Guide

**Keep**: 1-2 lands (W + G sources) + lifegain trigger + payoff creature
**Dream hand**: Plains + Forest + Soul Warden + Trelasarra + Conclave Mentor + Scurry Oak

**Mulligan**: No green or white source, all 3-drops, no lifegain triggers, 4+ lands

**Mulligan aggressively** — any 1-drop trigger + 2-drop payoff is a functional hand.
