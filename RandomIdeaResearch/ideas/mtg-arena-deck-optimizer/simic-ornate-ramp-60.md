# Simic Ornate Ramp (Historic Bo1)

**Format:** Historic | **Cards:** 60 | **Colors:** UG
**Archetype:** Ramp / Creature Flood
**Status:** Built from confirmed collection (untapped.gg export) + targeted crafts

## Why This Deck

Ornate Imitations is a digital-only bomb that conjures a random creature at every mana value from 1 to X. Cast it for X=6 and you get 6 creatures. Cast it for X=10 and you get 10. The strategy: ramp as fast as possible, cast Ornate Imitations for a massive X, then slam Craterhoof Behemoth to give everything +N/+N and trample for an instant kill.

**The ramp shell** is dense — 20 ramp pieces mean you're consistently hitting 7+ mana by turn 4. Dryad of the Ilysian Grove fixes all your colors and enables extra land drops. Nissa doubles your forests. Nyxbloom Ancient triples everything.

**The kill:** Ornate Imitations for X=6+ floods the board, then Craterhoof gives every creature +N/+N and trample where N = your creature count. Even random 1/1s become 8/8+ tramplers. Defense of the Heart can shortcut straight to Nyxbloom + Craterhoof against creature decks.

## The Dream Sequence

```
T1: Forest, Llanowar Elves
T2: Land, Lotus Cobra + Wolfwillow Haven (now at 4+ mana available)
T3: Fabled Passage (Cobra triggers for 2 mana), Dryad of the Ilysian Grove
    → now every land taps for any color, extra land drop available
T4: Nissa, Who Shakes the World → forests now tap for double
    → 5 forests = 10 green mana available
T5: Ornate Imitations for X=8 (8 creatures at MV 1-8)
    → Craterhoof Behemoth → +10/+10 trample to everything → lethal
```

With Nyxbloom Ancient in play, each land taps for triple. 5 forests = 15 mana. Ornate Imitations for X=13. That's 13 random creatures + your existing board + Craterhoof = absolute carnage.

## Gameplay Guide

### Mulligan
**Snap keep:** Forest + Llanowar Elves + any ramp spell + land
**Great:** Lotus Cobra + Fabled Passage + ramp pieces
**Good:** 2-3 lands + Wolfwillow Haven + any payoff (Ornate/Nissa/Defense)
**Keepable:** Slow hand with Cultivate + lands (you'll get there, just slower)
**Mulligan:** No green source, no ramp, all expensive cards, 5+ lands

### Sequencing Priority
1. **Turn 1:** Llanowar Elves (always)
2. **Turn 2:** Lotus Cobra > Wolfwillow Haven > Growth Spiral > Kinnan
3. **Turn 3:** Dryad of the Ilysian Grove > Cultivate > Nissa (if 5 mana)
4. **Turn 4-5:** Nissa (double forests) > Ornate Imitations for X=4+ > Defense of the Heart
5. **Turn 5+:** Ornate Imitations for max X, then Craterhoof

### When to Cast Ornate Imitations
- **X=4 minimum** — fewer than 4 creatures isn't worth the card
- **X=6+ is the sweet spot** — 6 random creatures is usually enough board presence to threaten lethal with Craterhoof next turn
- **X=8+ with Nissa/Nyxbloom** — this is game over, you'll have more creatures than they can handle
- **Hold Negate** when casting big Ornate Imitations — if it gets countered with all your mana spent, you lose

### Defense of the Heart Lines
Against creature decks (aggro/midrange with 3+ creatures):
- **Default tutor:** Nyxbloom Ancient + Craterhoof Behemoth
  - If you have ANY other creatures in play, Craterhoof kills them immediately
  - Nyxbloom sticks around to power future Ornate Imitations
- **If you already have Nyxbloom:** Craterhoof + Koma
- **If behind on board:** Koma + Craterhoof (Koma stabilizes, Craterhoof threatens)

### Against Specific Matchups

**vs Aggro (Mono-Red, White Weenie):**
- Survive early. Llanowar Elves blocks in a pinch. Shark Typhoon cycling makes a blocker.
- Defense of the Heart is amazing here — they always have 3+ creatures.
- You win if you reach turn 5 with any payoff.

**vs Midrange (Rakdos, Jund):**
- They'll try to pick off your ramp pieces. Wolfwillow Haven dodges creature removal.
- Koma is your best card — uncounterable, makes tokens, dominates the board.
- Ornate Imitations rebuilds after their removal.

**vs Control (Azorius, Dimir):**
- Hold Negate for their counterspells on your big turn.
- Shark Typhoon cycling can't be countered (it's a triggered ability).
- Bala Ged Recovery gets back a countered Ornate Imitations.
- Koma is uncounterable — their only answer is a board wipe.

**vs Combo:**
- You're slower than fast combo. Use Negate to disrupt their key spell.
- If you can land Defense of the Heart or Ornate Imitations first, you win on board presence.
