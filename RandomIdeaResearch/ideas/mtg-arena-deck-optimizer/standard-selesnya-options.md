## Option 1: Selesnya Enchantments (Value Grind)

The proven Standard GW archetype. Every enchantment you play grows Optimistic Scavenger, Caretaker's Talent draws you cards off creature ETBs, and Sheltered by Ghosts removes threats while being an enchantment trigger. Grindy, resilient, and hard to interact with once rolling.

### How It Works
```
TURN 1: Optimistic Scavenger or Novice Inspector
TURN 2: Sheltered by Ghosts (exile their creature, Scavenger grows)
         Hopeful Vigil (make a 1/1, Scavenger grows)
         Innkeeper's Talent on Scavenger (now 4/4+ with trample)
TURN 3: Caretaker's Talent (Scavenger grows AGAIN, now every creature ETB draws)
         Helpful Hunter (ETB draw + Caretaker investigates)
TURN 4+: Bury them in value. Every enchantment grows Scavenger.
          Every creature draws cards. Enduring Innocence draws on attacks.
```

## Option 2: Abzan Enchantments (GWb Splash)

Same enchantment core but splashing black for Deep-Cavern Bat (hand disruption) and better removal. Slightly more interactive, better against combo/control. This is your "GW with a little black" preference.

### How It Works
```
TURN 1: Optimistic Scavenger or Novice Inspector
TURN 2: Deep-Cavern Bat (take their best card, lifelink body)
         Sheltered by Ghosts (exile their creature + Scavenger grows)
         Mosswood Dreadknight (recursive 3/2 that keeps coming back)
TURN 3: Caretaker's Talent (card advantage engine)
         Go for the Throat (kill anything that moves)
TURN 4+: Grind. Dreadknight recurs, Bat strips answers, enchantments
          keep growing Scavenger. Hard to out-value.
```

## Playing for Achievements

Tips for grinding Standard achievements:
- **Daily wins**: Option 1 is faster for quick games — more consistent mana, gets on board early
- **"Cast X spells" achievements**: Both decks cast lots of cheap spells
- **"Win with X color" achievements**: Option 2 covers W, G, AND B in one deck
- **"Deal X damage" achievements**: Innkeeper's Talent on a pumped Scavenger closes fast
- **"Destroy X creatures"**: Option 2 with 4 direct removal spells + Sheltered exile

## Option 3: Selesnya Go-Wide (Bunnicorn Beats)

Built from your confirmed collection. Goes wide with tokens and permanents, then closes with Regal Bunnicorn whose power/toughness equals your nonland permanent count. Sheltered by Ghosts and Werefox Bodyguard handle removal while triggering Scavenger.

### How It Works
```
TURN 1: Optimistic Scavenger or Novice Inspector
TURN 2: Sheltered by Ghosts (exile their creature, Scavenger grows, ward 2)
         Hopeful Vigil (1/1 soldier + Scavenger grows)
         Innkeeper's Talent on Scavenger (now 4/4 with trample!)
TURN 3: Caretaker's Talent (every creature ETB = investigate = card draw)
         Werefox Bodyguard (flash, exiles their creature, enchantment = Scavenger grows)
         Carrot Cake (food + rabbit = 3 permanents for Bunnicorn count)
TURN 4+: Bunnicorn arrives as a 6/6+. Innkeeper's Talent on it = trample.
          Enduring Innocence draws cards on every attack.
          Virtue of Loyalty puts +1/+1 on everything every turn.
```

### What This Deck Does Well
- **Goes wide fast**: Carrot Cake tokens, Hopeful Vigil soldiers, Inspector clues, Caretaker clues — lots of permanents = massive Bunnicorn
- **Removal that grows you**: Sheltered by Ghosts exiles their best creature AND triggers Scavenger AND gives ward 2. Werefox flashes in as surprise exile
- **Card draw**: Caretaker's Talent + Helpful Hunter + Novice Inspector clues + Enduring Innocence combat draw = you never run out of gas
- **Resilient**: Enduring Innocence comes back from graveyard. Werefox flashes in at instant speed. Ward 2 from Sheltered protects your best creature
