# Etali's Casino — Temur Steal & Reveal (Historic Bo1)

**Format:** Historic | **Cards:** 60 | **Colors:** URG (splashing B)
**Archetype:** Ramp → Steal / Reveal / Chaos
**Status:** Built from confirmed collection — zero wildcards needed

## Why This Deck

Every single threat plays cards off the top of libraries — yours, your opponent's, or conjured out of thin air. You never know what you're getting. The game plan is "play their deck better than they do." Peak entertainment value.

## The Engine

```
RAMP PHASE (Turns 1-3):
  Llanowar Elves → Lotus Cobra → Worldweave
  → Every creature you cast seeks a land
  → Jump from 4 mana to 7+ by turn 4

CHAOS PHASE (Turns 4+):
  Etali, Primal Storm → attacks: exile top card of EACH player's library, cast free
  Gonti, Canny Acquisitor → steals from opponent's deck
  Goben, Gene-Splice Savant → gene-splices creatures into weird hybrids
  Bill Ferny → steals opponent's stuff
  Geological Appraiser → discover (random cascade)
  Chimil, the Inner Sun → discover 5 every end step
  One with the Multiverse → play your top card free forever

WORLDWEAVE AMPLIFIER:
  Every creature that enters (including stolen/conjured ones)
  → Worldweave seeks another land → more mana → bigger chaos
```

## How It Plays

### Turn 1: Llanowar Elves
Always. The entire deck needs early mana.

### Turn 2: Lotus Cobra or Fear of Change
- **Cobra**: Sets up explosive turn 3 with Fabled Passage triggers
- **Fear of Change**: Immediate conjure. The conjured creature does NOT trigger Worldweave (conjured, not cast)

### Turn 3: Worldweave or Cultivate
- **Worldweave**: Now every creature = bonus land. Engine online
- **Cultivate**: Fix colors for Etali/Gonti/Koma

### Turn 4-5: Chaos Begins
- Gonti steals their best card
- Geological Appraiser discovers into something random
- Fear of Change keeps upgrading your board with bigger conjured creatures
- Goben gene-splices things into bizarre hybrids
- You're ramping 1-2 extra lands per turn from Worldweave

### Turn 6+: Full Casino
- **Etali** attacks: flip the top of EVERY library, cast the best stuff free
- **Chimil** discovers 5 every end step — random haymakers for free
- **One with the Multiverse**: play your top card free + cast from hand free
- At this point you're casting 2-3 free spells per turn, most of them random
- Koma as the unkillable backup plan if the casino goes cold

### The Dream Sequence

```
Board: Worldweave + Lotus Cobra + Etali

Attack with Etali → exile top card of both libraries
  → You flip Ulamog from opponent's deck → cast free
  → Ulamog enters → Worldweave seeks a land → Cobra triggers
  → Your card: Geological Appraiser → cast free → discover 3
  → Discover hits something random → cast it free
  → That creature enters → Worldweave seeks ANOTHER land

Result: 3 free spells, 2 bonus lands, and you just cast their finisher
```

## Matchups

| Matchup | Plan |
|---------|------|
| **Aggro** | Llanowar + Cobra block early. Worldweave ramps past them. Gonti/Fear of Change create blockers. Koma stabilizes |
| **Control** | Chimil makes your spells uncounterable. One with the Multiverse grinds. Etali steals their answers. They can't keep up with free spells |
| **Midrange** | You go way bigger. Etali + Gonti steal their best cards and use them against them. Worldweave outmanas them |
| **Combo** | Race them with ramp. Gonti might steal their combo piece. Koma is uncounterable stabilizer |

## Mulligan Guide

**Keep**: 2-3 lands (including green) + Llanowar or Cobra + any ramp/Worldweave
**Dream hand**: Forest + Llanowar + Lotus Cobra + Fabled Passage + Worldweave + any chaos creature
**Mulligan**: No green source, no ramp, all 6+ drops, 5+ lands
