# Temur Worldweave Artifact Storm — Historic Bo1 (60 Cards)

**Format**: Historic Bo1
**Archetype**: Temur (U/R/G) Artifact Creature Storm
**Strategy**: Flood the board with free/cheap artifact creatures. Each one triggers Worldweave (ramp) and Impact Tremors (burn). Third Path Iconoclast turns free noncreature artifacts into tokens that ALSO trigger both engines. Build to a massive Ornate Imitations turn or Aetherflux Reservoir laser.

**Built from**: Your collection — zero wildcards needed for the core

---

## The Engine

```
THE STORM LOOP:

  Worldweave + Impact Tremors on board

  Cast Ornithopter (FREE)
  → Worldweave: seek a land onto battlefield
  → Impact Tremors: 1 damage to opponent
  → Springleaf Drum: tap Ornithopter for mana
  = 0 mana spent, +1 land, +1 damage, +1 mana floating

  Cast Bone Saw (FREE artifact)
  → Third Path Iconoclast creates 1/1 Soldier token
  → Token entering triggers Worldweave: seek ANOTHER land
  → Token entering triggers Impact Tremors: ANOTHER 1 damage
  = 0 mana spent, +1 land, +1 damage, +1 token

  With Foundry Inspector: Gingerbrute also costs 0
  → Same triggers: +1 land, +1 damage, +1 hasty creature

THE RAMP:
  Turn 3: Worldweave
  Turn 4: Cast 3-4 free/cheap creatures = seek 3-4 lands
  Turn 5: You're at 8-9 mana. Cast Ornate Imitations for X=6+
  → Conjure 6+ random creatures directly onto the BATTLEFIELD
  → Each one triggers Impact Tremors = 6+ damage instantly
  → Does NOT trigger Worldweave (conjured, not cast)
  → But you now have a massive board to swing with next turn

THE LASER:
  Aetherflux Reservoir: each spell cast gains increasing life
  1st spell = +1, 2nd = +2, 3rd = +3, 4th = +4...
  Cast 7 spells in a turn = +28 life total
  At 51+ life: pay 50, deal 50 damage. Game over.
  Free spells (Ornithopter, Bone Saw) count toward storm count!
```

## How It Plays

### Turn 1: Deploy cheap stuff
Ornithopter (free) + Springleaf Drum or Opt. You already have board presence and mana fixing.

### Turn 2: Engine creature or enchantment
- **Emporium Thopterist** (1U): Starts conjuring free Ornithopters next upkeep
- **Third Path Iconoclast** (UR): Now your Bone Saws and Opts create tokens
- **Impact Tremors** (1R): Every creature from here on deals damage

### Turn 3: Worldweave or Cabaretti Revels
- **Worldweave**: Ramp engine online. Every creature you cast = seek a land
- **Cabaretti Revels**: Cascade engine online. Every cheap creature you cast = potentially cheat a bigger one onto the board (+ Tremors ping)

### Turn 4: STORM TURN
With Worldweave + Tremors + Revels online:
- Cast Ornithopter (free) → Worldweave: seek land → Tremors: 1 dmg → Revels: reveal top, maybe free creature → Tremors: another 1 dmg
- Cast Bone Saw (free) → Iconoclast token → Tremors: 1 dmg
- Tap Ornithopter with Springleaf Drum for mana → cast more stuff
- You're ramping lands, dealing damage, AND cheating creatures onto board for nothing

### Turn 5-6: The Kill

**Path A — Ornate Imitations:**
With 8+ lands from Worldweave ramp, cast Ornate Imitations for X=6.
→ Conjure 6 random creatures (MV 1-6) directly onto the battlefield
→ Impact Tremors fires 6 times = 6 damage INSTANTLY
→ Board floods with 6 random creatures + your existing Thopters/tokens
→ Swing for lethal with the massive board

**Path B — Aetherflux Laser:**
Cast 7+ spells in a turn (easy with free artifacts):
Ornithopter (0) + Bone Saw (0) + Bone Saw (0) + Opt (1) + Ornithopter (0) + Springleaf Drum (1)
= 6 spells = +21 life from Aetherflux (plus any Revels/Tremors triggers)
Keep chaining free stuff until you hit 51 life → pay 50 = deal 50 damage

**Path C — Thopter Beatdown:**
Multiple Thopterists = multiple Ornithopters per turn, each gets +2/+0 per Thopterist.
2 Thopterists = Ornithopters are 4/2 fliers. 3+ per turn in the air adds up fast.

  │
  ├─→ Conjures Ornithopter each upkeep (FREE card every turn)

  │
  ├─→ Every creature you CAST = seek a land
  ├─→ Free creatures = free ramp
  ├─→ Does NOT trigger from Ornate Imitations (conjured, not cast)
  └─→ Gets you to Ornate Imitations mana FAST

IMPACT TREMORS (burn engine)
  │
  ├─→ Every creature ETB = 1 damage
  ├─→ Free creatures = free damage
  ├─→ Iconoclast tokens = extra damage
  └─→ Ornate Imitations X=8 = 8 creatures to board = 8 Tremors damage

THIRD PATH ICONOCLAST (token bridge)
  │
  ├─→ Noncreature spells → 1/1 token
  │     ├─→ Bone Saw (0) → free token → free Worldweave + Tremors trigger
  │     ├─→ Opt (1) → token + card
  │     └─→ Springleaf Drum → token (if cast, not just tapped)
  └─→ Bridges noncreature spells into the creature-ETB engines

ORNATE IMITATIONS (the payoff)
  │
  ├─→ Conjure random creature at MV 1, 2, 3... up to X onto BATTLEFIELD
  ├─→ Each creature entering triggers Impact Tremors (1 damage each)
  ├─→ Does NOT trigger Worldweave (conjured, not cast)
  ├─→ X=8 with Tremors = 8 damage + 8 random creatures on board
  └─→ Massive board flood for lethal combat swing

AETHERFLUX RESERVOIR (alt-win)
  │
  ├─→ Each spell = +N life (N = spells cast this turn)
  ├─→ Free spells count (Ornithopter, Bone Saw)
  └─→ At 51 life: pay 50 → deal 50 damage = instant kill

## What Makes This Different from the Bant Builds

| Bant Blink Builds | Temur Storm Build |
|---|---|
| Wins through blinking creatures for repeated ETBs | Wins through **casting many spells** in a single turn |
| Needs blink spells + blink targets in hand | Needs free/cheap artifacts + engine pieces on board |
| Reactive — holds up blink spells as protection | Proactive — dumps hand as fast as possible |
| Grinds value over many turns | Can kill in a single explosive turn |
| White for Cloudshift/Distinguished Conjurer | Red for Impact Tremors + Third Path Iconoclast |
| Ornate Imitations is a bonus | Ornate Imitations is THE finisher |
| No artifact synergy | Full artifact theme — free artifacts, cost reduction, Springleaf Drum |
| Omniscience as top-end | Aetherflux Reservoir as alt-win |

## Matchups

| Matchup | Plan |
|---------|------|
| **Aggro** | Ornithopter blocks fliers. Race with Impact Tremors — you deal passive damage every turn. Aetherflux lifegain keeps you alive. Cabaretti Revels can cheat a blocker onto the board |
| **Control** | Dump free stuff before they can counter. Tremors damage is hard to interact with (enchantment removal is rare in Historic). Bait counters on creatures, resolve Worldweave |
| **Midrange** | You go wider faster. Worldweave ramps past them. Tremors adds up. Ornate Imitations floods the board with more creatures than they can handle |
| **Combo** | Race. Your free spells let you deploy faster than most combo decks. Tremors provides a clock they can't ignore. Aetherflux threatens 50-damage laser |
