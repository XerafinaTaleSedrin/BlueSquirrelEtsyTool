## What Each Card Upgrades (by deck)

### Conjuration Game (Diamond push — highest priority)

| Card | Rarity | Impact |
|------|--------|--------|
| **Brainstorm** | Rare | Draw 3, put back 2. With Worldweave = 3 conjured cards for 1 mana. THE best cantrip in Historic |
| **Growth Spiral** | Rare | Instant ramp + draw. Replaces They Went This Way — faster, triggers Worldweave |
| **Teferi, Time Raveler** | Rare | Bounces threats, draws cards, makes your spells uncounterable. Protects your Omniscience turn |
| **Supreme Verdict** | Rare | Uncounterable board wipe. Keeps you alive against aggro |
| **Wilderness Reclamation** | Rare | Untaps all lands on end step. Doubles your mana. Cast interaction on their turn AND develop on yours |
| **Enter the Infinite** | Rare | With Omniscience: draw entire deck, Worldweave conjures everything, Aetherflux kills. Deterministic win |
| **Narset, Parter of Veils** | Uncommon | Digs for Omniscience, shuts down opponent card draw |

| **Shark Typhoon** | Rare | Cycle for draw (Worldweave trigger) + flying shark token. Great at every stage |
| **Mystical Dispute** | Uncommon | 1-mana counter vs blue spells, 3-mana counter vs anything. Cheap protection |
| **Dovin's Veto** | Uncommon | Uncounterable counter for noncreature spells. Protects Omniscience from removal |
| **Sterling Grove** | Rare | Protects Worldweave/Omniscience with shroud. Sac to tutor either one |
