## Pro Tips

### Best Times to Visit:
- **Saturday 9-10am**: Fresh stock from Friday night donations
- **Monday morning**: Weekend donations just processed
- **Avoid Sunday afternoons**: Picked over from weekend shoppers

### What to Bring:
- ✅ Tape measure (for furniture)
- ✅ Phone with eBay app (check sold listings)
- ✅ Reusable bags
- ✅ Cash (some stores discount for cash)
- ✅ This cheat sheet!

### Quick Profit Calculator:
**Good deal if**: (eBay sold price × 0.7) - purchase price > $10
- Example: Item sells for $50 on eBay
- After fees: $50 × 0.7 = $35
- Buy for: $5
- Profit: $30 ✅
