## 🗓️ BEST TIMES TO VISIT

### Saturday 9-10am
✅ **Best time overall**
- Fresh stock from Friday donations
- Beat weekend shoppers
- Staff just put out new items

### Monday Morning
✅ **Second best**
- Weekend donations processed
- Less crowded than Saturday
- Good for Budd Bay Bargains (weekday only)

### Weekday Mornings (Tue-Fri 9-11am)
✅ **Good for serious resellers**
- No weekend crowds
- More time to browse
- Can chat with staff about restock schedule

### Avoid:
❌ Sunday afternoons (picked over)
❌ Saturday afternoons (crowded + picked over)
❌ End of month (inventory low before restocking)
