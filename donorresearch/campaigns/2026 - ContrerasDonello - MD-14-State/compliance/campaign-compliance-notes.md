## Maryland-Specific Rules

### Contribution Limits

- The $6,000 limit applies to the **total** contributions from one donor during the 4-year election cycle
- This limit applies regardless of how many elections the candidate participates in during the cycle
- A person may make **unlimited aggregate contributions** to multiple committees, but not more than $6,000 to any single candidate committee

### Allowed Contributors (Unlike Federal)

Maryland permits contributions from:
- Individuals
- Corporations
- Labor unions
- PACs/Political committees
- LLCs (verify attribution rules)

### Disclosure Requirements

- Contributions of $51+ require disclosure
- Must report contributor name, address, and contribution amount
- Employers must be reported for contributions over certain threshold

### Reporting Schedule

| Report | Timing |
|--------|--------|
| Annual Report | January |
| Pre-Primary | Before primary election |
| Pre-General | Before general election |
| Post-Election | After election |
