## Detailed Positions

### Education

**Position:**
Fully implement the Blueprint for Maryland's Future. Support educators and ensure every student in District 14 has access to a world-class education, regardless of their background or zip code.

**Key Talking Points:**
- Blueprint for Maryland's Future implementation is critical
- Support for educators and teachers
- Equal access to quality education regardless of zip code
- Personal stake: Her children attend Montgomery County public schools

**Alignment Organizations:**
- Maryland State Education Association (MSEA)
- Montgomery County Education Association (MCEA)
- PTA/PTO organizations
- Education advocacy groups
- Teachers unions
