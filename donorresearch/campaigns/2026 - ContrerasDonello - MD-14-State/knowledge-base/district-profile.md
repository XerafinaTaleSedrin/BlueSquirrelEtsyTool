## 2026 Race Dynamics

### THE KEY FINDING: OPEN SEAT

**Delegate Pamela Queen is retiring** - She announced in September 2025 that she will not seek reelection. This creates an **open seat** opportunity.

### Current Incumbents

| Name | Status for 2026 | First Elected | Notes |
|------|-----------------|---------------|-------|
| **Anne Kaiser** | Running for reelection | 2002 | Former Majority Leader, Ways & Means Chair |
| **Bernice Mireku-North** | Running for reelection | Appointed 2023 | Seeking first full term; Ways & Means Committee |
| **Pamela Queen** | **RETIRING** | Appointed 2016 | Not running; open seat |

### The "District 14 Team"

Kaiser and Mireku-North are running as a coordinated team with Senator Zucker. They have endorsements from:
- Congressman Jamie Raskin (MD-8)
- Former County Executive Ike Leggett

### Known Primary Opponents (as of January 2026)

| Candidate | Background | Strengths | Concerns |
|-----------|------------|-----------|----------|
| **Matt Post** | Former student school board member; Yale Law grad; MSEA legal staffer | Young, education focus, gun control advocacy, works for teachers union | Insider with MSEA; first-announced candidate |

**Filing Deadline:** February 24, 2026
**Primary:** June 23, 2026
**General Election:** November 3, 2026
