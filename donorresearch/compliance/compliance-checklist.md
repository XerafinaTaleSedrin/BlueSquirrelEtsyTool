# Campaign Finance Compliance Checklist

*Use before making contact or solicitation recommendations*

---

## Pre-Research Compliance Check

Before adding a prospect to the research pipeline:

### Eligibility Verification

- [ ] **US Citizen or Permanent Resident**
  - Foreign nationals cannot contribute to federal campaigns
  - Green card holders CAN contribute

- [ ] **Not a Federal Contractor**
  - Corporations with federal contracts cannot contribute
  - Employees of contractors CAN contribute personally
  - Verify if prospect's company has federal contracts

- [ ] **Legal Age**
  - Must be 18+ to contribute
  - Exception: Minors can contribute if decision is truly theirs

- [ ] **Not a Prohibited Entity**
  - Not a corporation contributing directly
  - Not a union contributing directly
  - Not a national bank

## Pre-Solicitation Compliance Check

Before recommending an ask amount:

### Contribution Capacity

- [ ] **Check FEC Records**

## Special Situations Checklist

### LLC Contributions

- [ ] **Verify LLC Structure**
  - Single-member LLC: Treated as individual (uses owner's limits)
  - Multi-member LLC: Must be attributed to members
  - LLC with corporate member: Cannot contribute

- [ ] **Document LLC Details**
  - LLC name: ______
  - Type: [ ] Single-member [ ] Multi-member [ ] Corporate member
  - If multi-member, attribution plan: ______

### Joint Contributions (Married Couples)

- [ ] **Separate Limits Apply**
  - Each spouse has own $6,600 limit
  - Joint account contribution split 50/50 unless specified

- [ ] **Document Attribution**
  - Spouse 1 amount: $______
  - Spouse 2 amount: $______

### Bundler Contributions

- [ ] **Track Bundler Attribution**
  - Bundler name: ______
  - Total bundled this period: $______
  - Threshold for disclosure ($18,200): [ ] Above [ ] Below

- [ ] **Individual Contributor Compliance**
  - Each bundled contribution must comply with limits
  - Each contributor's information required if $200+

## Red Flag Checklist

### Immediate Stops

If any of these are true, **do not solicit**:

- [ ] Foreign national (no green card)
- [ ] Federal contractor (corporate contribution)
- [ ] Already maxed out to our campaign
- [ ] On OFAC sanctions list
- [ ] Under criminal indictment for relevant offense

### Requires Verification

If any of these apply, **verify before soliciting**:

- [ ] LLC contribution (verify structure)
- [ ] Cash contribution over $100 (not permitted)
- [ ] Contribution from business account (verify entity type)
- [ ] Contribution near or at limits (verify exact amounts)
- [ ] Multiple family members giving (verify not straw donors)

### Document and Monitor

Note these in prospect file:

- [ ] Works in sensitive industry
- [ ] Past legal issues (resolved)
- [ ] Complex business structures
- [ ] History of supporting opponents

---

## Solicitation Compliance

### During the Ask

- [ ] **Never suggest circumventing limits**
  - Don't suggest contributions in others' names
  - Don't suggest corporate reimbursement
  - Don't suggest structuring

- [ ] **Accurate Representation**
  - Contribution is to [Campaign Committee Name]
  - FEC ID: [Number]
  - Contributions are not tax deductible

### After Commitment

- [ ] **Collect Required Information**
  - For contributions $200+: Name, address, occupation, employer
  - Document how information was obtained

- [ ] **Provide Receipt**
  - Written acknowledgment of contribution
  - Include required campaign committee information

---

## 48-Hour Reporting Check

### Within 20 Days of Election

If contribution is:
- [ ] $1,000 or more
- [ ] Received within 20 days of election

Then:
- [ ] Must report to FEC within 48 hours
- [ ] Notify compliance/finance team immediately

---

## Post-Contribution Checklist

### For Finance Team

- [ ] Verify contribution within limits
- [ ] Verify contributor information complete
- [ ] Deposit within [timeframe per campaign policy]
- [ ] Issue acknowledgment
- [ ] Enter in compliance database
- [ ] Track against limits

### If Excessive Contribution Received

- [ ] Do NOT deposit over-limit portion
- [ ] Contact contributor within 60 days
- [ ] Options:
  - Refund excessive amount
  - Reattribute to another individual
  - Redesignate to different election
- [ ] Document resolution
