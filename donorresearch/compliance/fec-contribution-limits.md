# FEC Contribution Limits

*Quick reference for federal campaign contribution limits*

**Cycle:** 2023-2024
**Last Updated:** [Date]

---

## Individual Contribution Limits

### To Candidates

| To | Per Election | Primary + General |
|----|--------------|-------------------|
| Candidate Committee | $3,300 | $6,600 |

**Note:** Primary and general elections count as separate elections with separate limits.

### To Other Committees

| To | Per Year | Per Cycle |
|----|----------|-----------|
| National Party Committee | $41,300 | $82,600 |
| State/District/Local Party | $10,000 combined | $20,000 combined |
| PAC (traditional) | $5,000 | $10,000 |
| PAC (Super PAC) | Unlimited | Unlimited |

## PAC Contribution Limits

### Multicandidate PAC to Candidates

| To | Per Election |
|----|--------------|
| Candidate Committee | $5,000 |

*A PAC becomes "multicandidate" after receiving contributions from 50+ people and contributing to 5+ candidates*

### Non-Multicandidate PAC to Candidates

| To | Per Election |
|----|--------------|
| Candidate Committee | $3,300 |

## Special Limits

### Cash Contributions
- Maximum cash contribution: **$100**
- All contributions over $100 must be by check, credit card, or other traceable method

### Anonymous Contributions
- Maximum anonymous contribution: **$50**
- Must be used for campaign expenses only (cannot be deposited to federal account)

---

## Prohibited Contributions

The following **cannot** contribute to federal campaigns:

| Prohibited Source | Notes |
|------------------|-------|
| Foreign nationals | Non-US citizens without green cards |
| Federal contractors | Corporations with federal contracts (individuals can give) |
| National banks | |
| Corporations | Directly (can fund PACs) |
| Labor unions | Directly (can fund PACs) |
| Contributions in another's name | Straw donor prohibition |
| Minors | Special rules apply |

## Contribution Timeline

### Designation

| Timing | Default Designation |
|--------|---------------------|
| Before primary | Primary election |
| After primary, before general | General election |
| After general | Next election cycle |

Contributors can designate contributions to specific elections regardless of timing.

### Excessive Contributions

If a contribution exceeds limits:
- Must be refunded, reattributed, or redesignated within **60 days**
- Campaign must make "best efforts" to resolve

---

## Reporting Thresholds

### Contributor Information Required

| Amount | Required Information |
|--------|---------------------|
| Under $50 | None |
| $50 - $199 | Name, address (best efforts) |
| $200+ | Name, address, occupation, employer |

### 48-Hour Reporting

Contributions of **$1,000+** received within **20 days** of an election must be reported within **48 hours**.

---

## Bundler Disclosure

If campaign uses bundlers who aggregate **$18,200+** in a covered period:
- Must disclose bundler identity
- Must report in FEC filings

## Quick Reference Card

### For Donor Research

When recommending ask amounts:

| Scenario | Maximum Ask |
|----------|-------------|
| New donor (no prior gifts this cycle) | $6,600 ($3,300 primary + $3,300 general) |
| Gave to primary only | $3,300 (general only) |
| Gave to general only | $3,300 (primary only) |
| Maxed to both | $0 (explore PAC, bundling, or future cycle) |

### For Bundlers

- Track all bundled contributions
- Prepare for bundler disclosure if threshold met
- Attribute contributions to bundler in records
